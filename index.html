Umunyeshuri wa Kaminuza y'u Rwanda GASASIRA Ange Felix yagiye mu Bwongereza mu marushanwa y'ubwiza

Umunyeshuri wa Kaminuza y'u Rwanda witwa GASASIRA Ange Felix yerekeje mu gihugu cy'u Bwongereza aho agiye guhagararira u Rwanda mu marushanwa mpuzamahanga y'ubwiza. Gasasira, wiga mu ishami ry'itangazamakuru, yavuze ko aya ari amahirwe akomeye yo guhagararira igihugu no kugaragaza ubushobozi bw'urubyiruko rw'u Rwanda ku rwego rw'isi.

Yakomeje avuga ko uru rugendo rugaragaza ko urubyiruko rufite ubushobozi bwo kwitwara neza no kuzamura isura y'igihugu mu ruhando mpuzamahanga.
